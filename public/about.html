The Story Behind Pragmatic Step

Not studied.
Built.

Pragmatic Step was not born in a classroom or a consulting firm. It was built across 20 years of real commercial pressure — winning architect endorsements in competitive markets, defending specifications against substitution at tender, building national sales teams that understood the difference between hunting and farming.

From the ground floor as a Sales Executive at Saint-Gobain Gyproc in 2005 — managing South Delhi, Noida, and Ghaziabad, leading specification efforts for Commonwealth Games stadiums — to National Sales Manager at Belgotex India, leading Pan India strategy for a Belgium MNC.

Every stage of that journey produced a lesson. Every lesson is now part of the program.

— Rajneesh Srivastava, Founder
