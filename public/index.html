The Stakeholder Ecosystem

Four stakeholders. One decision chain.
Win them all.

Each holds a different power at a different moment in the project cycle.

🏛
Stakeholder 01
Architect / Designer
Writes the specification. Highest influence at design stage. Technical authority and trust built over time make specification the natural outcome.
📋
Stakeholder 02
PMC / Consultant
Controls specification on large projects. Approves equivalents. The silent authority who can confirm or unravel your position at any stage.
🏗
Stakeholder 03
Contractor / Applicator
Executes the purchase. Can substitute without protection. The most common leakage point between specification written and revenue received.
🏢
Stakeholder 04
Client / Developer
The ultimate authority. Can override everyone. Rarely engaged by specification teams — the most costly miss in project sales.
